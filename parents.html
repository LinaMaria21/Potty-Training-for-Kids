Potty Time Hero — For Parents

What it is: A friendly app that reminds small children to take potty breaks and rewards them when they do.

How it works:

  • App plays a short tune or message when it’s potty time.
  • Child presses the big "I went potty!" button after success.
  • The app gives a small reward (a sticker, animation, or voice praise).

Why we built it: To make potty learning fun and encourage regular breaks from play so accidents are less likely.

Notes for parents:

  • All content is simple and kid-friendly.
  • Settings (times, sounds) would be in the hidden parent panel in the full app.
  • This demo is a prototype to show the idea. No personal data is collected in this prototype.
